Title: Dongfang Electric's share price rose after securing a core equipment order for a 50 MW combined-cycle power generation project in Kazakhstan
Publisher: Zhitong Finance / Futunn
Date: 2025-12-12
URL: https://news.futunn.com/en/post/66105604/dongfang-electric-s-share-price-rose-after-securing-a-core

Key points:
- Dongfang Electric said its self-developed F-class G50 heavy-duty gas turbine secured a core equipment order in Kazakhstan (Zhambyl region).
- The report describes this as the first complete-unit export of China's heavy-duty gas turbine.
- Tianfeng commentary in the same article mentions product roadmap expansion: G15, G80, and G200.
